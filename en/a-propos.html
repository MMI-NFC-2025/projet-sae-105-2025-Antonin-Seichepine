About

A project of memory and light.
Born from Lise Meitner’s story, Lumina highlights the legacy left by women in science.

The spirit of the project

Created within the MMI department in Montbéliard, Lumina is a project blending science communication and engagement. Its goal is to shine a light on forgotten researchers, their discoveries, and the values they pass on today, drawing on the journey of Lise Meitner.

Other projects

This website is part of a series of student projects focused on major scientific figures and knowledge sharing.
