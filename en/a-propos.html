A propos

A project of memory and light.
Born from the story of Lise Meitner, Lumina highlights the legacy left by women in science.

The spirit of the project

Created within the MMI department of Montbéliard, Lumina blends science communication and advocacy. Its goal is to shine a light on forgotten women researchers, their discoveries, and the values they still pass on today, drawing on the journey of Lise Meitner.

Other projects

This site is part of a series of student projects focused on major scientific figures and the transmission of knowledge.
