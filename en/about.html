About

A project of memory and light.
Born from the story of Lise Meitner, Lumina highlights the legacy left by women in science.

Project spirit

Created within the MMI department of Montbéliard, Lumina blends science communication and commitment. Its goal is to shine a light on forgotten women researchers, their discoveries, and the values they still inspire today, drawing on the journey of Lise Meitner.

Other projects

This site is part of a series of student projects focused on major scientific figures and on passing knowledge forward.
